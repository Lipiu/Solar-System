About the Planets

Explore our solar system, fun facts about each planet!

Mercury

The smallest planet and closest to the Sun. A day here is longer than its year!

Venus

The hottest planet, its thick clouds trap heat in a runaway greenhouse effect.

Earth

Our home, the only known planet with liquid water and life. And javascript unfortunetly.

Mars

The “Red Planet”, home to Olympus Mons, the tallest volcano in the solar system.

Jupiter

The largest planet, its Great Red Spot is a giant storm bigger than Earth.

Saturn

Famous for its stunning rings made of ice and rock, the solar system’s beauty! Slay!

Uranus

Rotates on its side! Scientists think a collision long ago knocked it over. Very cool!

Neptune

The windiest planet, wind blows faster than 2.000 km/h!

Pluto

Once the ninth planet, now a dwarf planet with a heart-shaped glacier, but we love her!
